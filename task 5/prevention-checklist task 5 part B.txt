# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)

##  Safety Tips

*  Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
*  Avoid clicking on unknown links or downloading attachments from suspicious emails or messages.
*  Keep your social media accounts private and limit sharing of personal information like phone number, address, or college details.
*  Always verify job/internship offers before applying—many scams target students with fake opportunities.
*  Do not use public Wi-Fi for logging into sensitive accounts like banking or email.
*  Regularly update your apps, phone software, and antivirus to stay protected from security threats.
*  Be cautious while sharing photos/videos online—once uploaded, they can be misused or edited.

##  UPI & Financial Safety (Important)

*  Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank.
*  Always check the receiver’s name before sending money via UPI apps (like Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm).
*  Remember: Receiving money does NOT require entering a PIN—only sending money does.

##  Reporting Cyber Crime

*  Report cyber fraud or suspicious activity at: **https://cybercrime.gov.in**
*  Helpline Number: **1930** (for immediate financial fraud assistance)

---

##  Quick Reminder

Stay alert, think before you click, and never trust unknown sources online. Your awareness is your best protection.
