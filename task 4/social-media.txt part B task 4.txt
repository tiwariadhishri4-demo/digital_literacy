##Responsible Social Media Use Checklist (College Students)
 
 DO'S
 1.Think before posting — ensure your content is appropriate and respectful
 2.Maintain privacy settings to protect personal information
 3.Use social media for learning, networking, and skill development
 4.Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation
 5.Be respectful and positive in comments and interactions

 Don’ts
 1.Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
 2.Avoid posting offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content
 3.Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
 4.Avoid wasting excessive time scrolling and neglecting studies
 5.Do not believe or spread fake news without checking sources
